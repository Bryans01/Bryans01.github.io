The goblin who found a red gem

Once upon a time, there was a small goblin named Gogor who lived deep in the forest. He was always the loneliest of all the goblins, as the other goblins were always busy playing games or searching for food. One day, Gogor decided to go on an adventure. He ventured deep into the forest, and soon he stumbled upon a sparkling red gem. He knew it must be rare and valuable, but he had no idea what to do with it. He decided to hide the gem in a nearby tree and return home. When Gogor returned home, he told his family about the gem he had found. His family was amazed at the discovery and urged Gogor to keep the gem safe. Gogor decided to keep the gem hidden in his pocket, but he was so excited about his discovery that he couldn’t help but show it to everyone he met. Soon, word spread throughout the forest of Gogor’s discovery. The other goblins were curious and wanted to see the gem for themselves. Gogor was very reluctant to show anyone the gem, as he was afraid it would be stolen. One day, Gogor was approached by a mysterious figure who offered to buy the gem from him. Gogor was hesitant at first, but the figure promised him a fortune in return. After much deliberation, Gogor sold the gem to the figure and was given a large sum of gold coins. Gogor was now a rich goblin. He shared his fortune with his family and used it to build a new home. He also donated some of his money to the poorer goblins in the forest. Gogor’s tale spread far and wide, and he was known throughout the land as the goblin who found the red gem. He was respected by all, and his discovery even made him a legend. Gogor went on to live a long and prosperous life, having made the most out of his one lucky find. The End.
